About The Event

Prepare to immerse yourself in the Compact Datathon by Mobius Club! Delve into the intricate world of data analysis, preprocessing, and modeling, where your mathematical prowess will be put to the test. Join us in formulating innovative solutions and unlocking new possibilities at the intersection of technology and mathematics.

Where

National Higher School Of Mathematics, Mahelma, Algiers

When

Wednesday to Friday
15-17 May
